For the Love of Fantasy

   FOR THE LOVE OF FANTASY is part of the Monopoly Events family of pop
   culture conventions, attracting thousands of fans annually, our next
   event takes place on the weekend of 22nd-23rd August 2026 in the ExCeL
   London.


   If you love fantasy movies and tv series then this is THE SHOW for you!


   You can expect to see guests from some of these fantastic franchises!
   Lord of the Rings, Supernatural, Stranger Things, The Vampire Diaries,
   Harry Potter, True Blood, Fantastic Beasts, Twilight, Wednesday, The
   Originals, Game of Thrones, Grimm, Teen Wolf, The Sandman, Buffy The
   Vampire Slayer, Lucifer, Angel, Highlander, Once Upon A Time, The
   Chronicles of Narnia, The Lost Boys and much, much more.


   Expect an all star line up of actors you love, amazing interactive set
   and prop builds, cosplay, immersive gaming, and tens of thousands of
   like minded fans!

   ​

   Transport yourself to a world of Vampires, Werewolves, wizards, orcs,
   witches, hobbits, demons, monsters and other supernatural favourites
   this summer!


   To view our current guest line up for our next show, visit the GUESTS
   page.
