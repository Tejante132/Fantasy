Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my fantasy team?

   Creating a team is simple! Once you choose a sport and contest, select
   players based on their form, performance, and upcoming matches. Set
   your captain and vice-captain wisely, as they earn extra points.

How are points calculated?

   Points are awarded based on real-world performances. Players earn
   points for scoring, assists, tackles, saves, and other key actions
   depending on the sport. Check the detailed scoring system within the
   platform to maximize your strategy.

Can I edit my team after selecting players?

   Yes, you can make changes to your team before the match starts.
   However, once the game begins, your selected lineup is locked in.

Is the platform available on mobile devices?

   Absolutely! You can play on both desktop and mobile. For the best
   experience, download the app and access all features on the go.

How do I improve my ranking?

   Winning requires a mix of strategy and insight. Stay updated on player
   stats, analyze past performances, and adjust your team accordingly. The
   more you refine your picks, the better your chances of climbing the
   leaderboard!

   Bringing the excitement of fantasy sports to every fan! Create your

   one place.
