Word History

   Note: Compare fancy entry 2, in Middle English a rare variant of
   fantasie; the two split from each other in early Modern English, so
   that fancy entry 2 and fantasy now differ in meaning and construction.
   In the sixteenth and seventeenth centuries a not infrequent sense,
   usually with the spelling phantasy, was "the formation of images or
